Komdak

ABOUT



"Komdak" is a retrofuturist space sci-fi fantasy comedy RPG that apparently nobody has ever heard of. Reconstructed from scattered source documents I sourced from a friend, it is an irreverant tabletop game seemingly developed in the late 90's. Included below are the various piecemeal resources I have made to allow my party and I to play it. It is every genre imaginable
